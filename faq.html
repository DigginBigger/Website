Common Questions

What about cancellations or rescheduling?

For any reason, cancellations before 48 hours notice will be fully refunded.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of my event?

We reserve the right to cancel any delivery if the temperature is below 40°F or if the weather conditions are unsafe for equipment use — including excessive winds, lightning, or heavy rain. Safety is our top priority, and all event decisions are made with safety as the first consideration. If rain begins after setup, the equipment will be temporarily packed up until it’s safe to resume. Once conditions improve, the trailer can be reconfigured and operated again. Please note that customers will not receive a refund if rain occurs after delivery and setup. However, we will contact you in advance to discuss options if weather appears to be a potential factor before your event.
