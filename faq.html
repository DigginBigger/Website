Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What about cancellations or rescheduling?

For any reason, cancellations 48 hours in advance will be fully refunded. We will try to reschedule if possible.

What happens if the weather is bad on the day of my event?

If rain begins after setup, the equipment will be temporarily packed up until it's safe to resume. Refunds aren't administed due to instanced like this.

What is needed for event setup?

Appropriate approval to enter and be situated at said propery for duraion of the requested times. Clear paths and space to accommodate a trailer that is 10 ft wide and 20ft long. No external power is required for operations.

Can this be an indoor event?

Yes, our units can be set up indoors or outdoors. Ceiling height should be at least 12 feet high. A school, church gymnasium or auditorium provides plenty of room. The trailer can be set up inside as long as the path can accommodate the 10ft x 20ft trailer and the play area of 20ft x 20ft.

Is it kid‑friendly?

Yes — we recommend 6 years of age or older, unless accompanied by an adult during operation of equipment.
